  Services Offered


        Information Management/ Publication. The Office of Public Affairs in coordination with the Office of the Secretary-General (OSG) ensures the proper, efficient and timely dissemination of information about the events, programs and achievements of the University to the general public through various media forms. It coordinates with the different offices for the gathering of complete and accurate details of information for public dissemination. It produces the Academia, the UST brochure, freshmen guide, freshmen campus tour, special University events such as the visit of the Pope, quadricentennial celebration, and other events, and the regular press releases for various University events and activities.

        University Campus Tour and VIP Guest Management. Campus Tour is provided by the Office of Public Affairs to various guests and visitors of the University. The guests may be groups from local and foreign educational institutions, alumni, and walk-in guests. During the visit of very important guests, OPA, in close coordination with OSG ensures that protocol is observed, takes charge of the itinerary and publicity of the event.

        Media Relations/Publicity. The Office of Public Affairs takes charge of the public relations program of the University. It works towards ensuring good relations with media organizations and personalities to facilitate publicity efforts through various media forms.

        Event Management. In coordination with the other entities in the University, the OPA conducts a pre-event preparation for events assigned to it, or the events that it organizes in relation to its function, assists in the event proper, and conducts the post-event evaluation.

More About OPA

The Office of Public Affairs was created in 1965 with the primary function of spearheading the publicity efforts of all faculties, colleges, departments and institutes of the University and taking charge of special publications such as souvenir programs, handbooks, leaflets and other related materials. The first director was Prof. Ernesto Franco..

- Office of Public Affairs
